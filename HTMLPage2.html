Salalah Glory Tours
Salalah Glory Tours was founded 2017 based in the city of Salalah by group of local touring experts as a travel guide and tour operator in the whole country of Oman which meets all tourists and travelers needs and fulfill their aspirations. Glory Tours Salalah organizes and handles different touring styles in Salalah and beyond starting at quick layovers form Salalah Airport to discover best highlights of the city, Shore excursions tours form Salalah Port and also day tours from your residency in Salalah in addition to transfer and taxi service Glory Tours is a local business offering a wide range of day tours and services for the discriminating traveler. We cater to individuals as well as groups who are interested in exploring unique travel experiences offered by Oman. All our tours are carefully planned to give visitors a completely unforgettable experience in order to introduce them to the history, culture, wildlife and the most beautiful scenery of Oman. However, we can create and facilitate any tours as required. All our transportation is either by luxury coach or 4WD vehicles. This enables us to reach the all the highlights and remotest areas of the Sultanate of Oman.
